About Our Firm

B. K. DE & Co., Chartered Accountants

Established in 1985, B. K. DE & Co. has grown to become one of Kolkata's trusted chartered accountancy firms. With nearly four decades of professional excellence, we provide comprehensive financial services to a diverse clientele ranging from small businesses to large corporations.

Our mission is to deliver exceptional professional services while maintaining the highest standards of integrity, technical competence, and ethical conduct as mandated by the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India.
